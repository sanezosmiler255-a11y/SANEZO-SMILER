### What is SANEZO-SMILER?
SANEZO-SMILER is a WhatsApp bot built with Node.js and Baileys.  
It helps you chat with AI, download videos, make stickers, and manage groups easily.

---

### ✨ Features

- **AI Chat** – Talk to AI right inside WhatsApp
- **Downloader** – Download videos and music from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram
- **Sticker Maker** – Turn photos and videos into stickers
- **Group Tools** – Anti-link, welcome message, tag all, promote, kick
- **Fun** – Memes, jokes, quotes, games
- **Utilities** – Weather, screenshot web, QR code, text to speech
- **Always Online** – Runs 24/7 when deployed properly
